The NFL Super Bowl

The Origins

The National Football League has been around for 98 years; however, the Super Bowl is a somewhat new game. The Super Bowl is currently the highest level of play in the NFL, and the people who play are decided by the best teams per division. Prior to the Super Bowl, the highest game was the Championship game. In 1967, the NFL decided that they would make the Championship game into the AFL-NFL World Championship Game. It was like this for three years until the leagues merges. When the AFL and NFL merged in 1970, the concepts changed. They decided to make the Super Bowl between the AFC Champion and the NFC Champion.

The Profit

The Super Bowl has greatly changed since its founding in the mid-20th century. At first it started as a simple game played between the best teams in the league; but, it has transformed into a money making paradise for NFL and other advertisers alike.

The Winners

Some teams have been specifically better than others in the Big Game, too. Teams like the 49ers boast records of 5-1, while others, like the Bills, have been there four times but have yet to be successful in getting the Lombardi Trophy.

The Best of the Best

There is also a prestigiuos award that comes with winning winnig the Super Bowl, a Super Bowl MVP. The best performing player in the Big Game is given the Super Bowl MVP Award.
